## 📖 About Tekken 5

**Tekken 5** is a fighting game developed and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Released as the fifth main installment in the iconic Tekken series, the game continues the intense Mishima family saga while introducing enhanced graphics, smoother gameplay, and a diverse selection of characters.

The game features a compelling story mode, arcade battles, multiplayer versus matches, and a variety of unlockable content that keeps players engaged for countless hours.

Tekken 5 is widely regarded as one of the greatest fighting games ever created due to its balanced combat system and extensive character roster.

## 🕹️ Gameplay Overview

In **Tekken 5**, players select fighters from a diverse cast of martial artists, each featuring unique fighting styles, combos, and special moves.

Popular game modes include:

* **Story Mode** – Experience character-specific storylines.
* **Arcade Mode** – Battle a series of opponents to reach the final boss.
* **Versus Mode** – Compete against friends locally.
* **Time Attack** – Defeat opponents as quickly as possible.
* **Survival Mode** – Fight endless opponents with limited health.

The game's refined mechanics and fluid controls make every battle exciting and strategic.
